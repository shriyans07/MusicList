Phonk

Phonk is a subgenre of hip-hop that emerged in the 1990s, known for its distinctive blend of hip-hop, Memphis rap, and chopped and screwed music. It is characterized by its dark, gritty, and lo-fi production, incorporating heavy basslines, haunting samples, and slowed-down tempos. Phonk often carries a nostalgic and underground vibe, offering a unique blend of atmospheric soundscapes and a raw, edgy aesthetic that captivates listeners who appreciate its distinct fusion of musical elements.
